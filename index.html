Treatments

Stratford House Dental Practice offer a comprehensive range of treatments to keep your smile healthy and pain free in a patient-focused, relaxed and friendly environment. If you have neglected your teeth up until now, we will not pass judgement, but will help restore your oral health.

Preventative Dental Care

Regular dental check-ups are the only way to ensure optimum dental health and prevent major dental problems from developing.

Cavity Treatment

A range of restorative treatments to deal with tooth decay, including fillings and root canals, to help to restore your dental health.

Hygiene Services

Regular professional dental cleaning will help to keep your mouth healthy, improve appearance and give you fresh breath and confidence.

Periodontal Treatment

Treatment to help cure gum disease, including scaling of the roots and advanced surgical deep cleaning around the infected tissues.

Crowns & Veneers

We offer solutions to improve the appearance of your smile using Veneers or Crowns, using the latest techniques and materials.

Dental Implants

Treatment to replace a tooth, lost through an accident or due to severe dental decay, using a titanium rod attached to a false tooth.

Dental Fees

Stratford House Dental Practice offer a full range of NHS dental services as well as a choice of private and cosmetic dental treatments. We also offer a number of alternative ways to finance private or cosmetic dental work, to make obtaining the dental health your need easier to afford.

  • NHS Dental Care
  • Private Dental Care
  • Dental Finance Schemes
  • Code Dental Plan
  • Medenta Finance

NHS Dental Care

All the treatment that your dentist believes is necessary to achieve and maintain good oral health is available on the NHS. This includes any treatment you need to keep your mouth, teeth and gums healthy and free of pain, but excludes any purely cosmetic treatment. Depending on what you need to have done, you should only ever be asked to pay one charge for each complete course of treatment within three bands of treatment.

Private Dental Care

Stratford House Dental Practice also provide our patients with high quality private dental care. This can be offered on an ongoing basis, or it may be used for one-off procedures not covered by NHS treatment, or where a better cosmetic appearance is desired than is offered.

Dental Finance Schemes

We also offer other ways to finance private dentistry. Details of each scheme can be found using the links on the right.

Code Dental Plan

To help spread the cost of your essential dental care, we are pleased to offer you membership of our new monthly dental plan, which includes up to 2 check-ups, up to 2 hygiene appointments for cleaning and polishing and all necessary intra-oral x-rays. Please ask at reception for full details.

Medenta Finance

If you are considering having any cosmetic, aesthetic, orthodontic or dental implant work and are not sure how you will afford the cost of the treatment, there is another solution.
